About Spectronic Technology

Spectronic Technology Sdn Bhd was established in 1997 with a mission to provide solutions for Printed Circuit Assemblers in Singapore, Malaysia, and Indonesia.

At Spectronic Technology, we manufacture Laser cut and Nickel electroformed stencils for solder paste printing on printed circuit boards. We also manufacture jigs and fixtures for manufacturing industries.
